Robert Szafarczyk

I am a Ph.D. student at the University of Glasgow. My work focuses on compilers and programming models for heterogeneous architectures. I am particularly interested in spatial dataflow architectures (e.g. FPGAs, CGRAs), and High-Level Synthesis.
